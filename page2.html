Lunar Terrain

Bringing some light to the dark side of the moon, this terrain map features an elevation hillshade of the lunar surface. Using elevation and lunar landing data from NASA, the surface of the moon is depicted here as an alien world on which probes and human beings alike have landed and explored. Emphasizing both natural features (mare and highlands) and humanoid landing spots (manned and unmanned) this map seeks to marry this otherworld with the human endeavor.
