Glacial Remnants in Wisconsin

Known for its landscape formed from glacial activity some thousands of years ago, Wisconsin is home to many peculiar landforms. This map illustrates some of these "curiosities". A blend of hillshade and tree cover, the emphasis of this map is on the landforms created as a result of glacial journeys during the last Ice Age.
