Web Map of Nightlife Transportation in Hong Kong

Hong Kong has incredibly robust public transportation infrastructure. This map displays one type of such transportation, public bus stops, in relation to student dormitories and bars in Hong Kong. Created to better understand some safer options for students to travel throughout the area while going to and from bars, the vibrant colors of this map reflect the atmosphere of Hong Kong nightlife. Feel free to turn datasets on and off to get a feel for the area itself and also the transportation scene.
