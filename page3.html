Terrain Map of Wisconsin's Forests and Parks

Created to shed some light on some state parks and forests of Wisconsin, this map aims to portray the magnitude of the state's forested areas. By featuring a hillshade and tree cover of the state, it becomes obvious that the majority of Wisconsin is not metropolitan, but rather offers beautiful opportunities to explore nature. While not frequented in the same way as national parks out west, Wisconsin's forests and parks hold their own.
